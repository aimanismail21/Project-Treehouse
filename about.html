Our Story

As Vancouverites, we have seen that homelessness in Vancouver is a problem that people of many demographics go through. This problem is wide-spread and affects even youth. Our mission is to help youth in Vancouver find temporary housing. This was inspired by Canadians opening their homes to Syrian refugees when the Canadian government was unable to house migrants.
