Frequently Asked Questions


How does volunteering and hosting work?

To volunteer and host a youth, you submit your home stay details. A social worker will contact you when match is made.

  • The details of your home stay will allow the social worker to narrow down the best possible match.
  • You may update your details at any time.
  • You may turn off your home stay availability when you want.
  • You can decline to host when a match is made.

Can I host?

Hosting is a generous gesture. Everyone that is interested in hosting a youth may do so. There are some conditions.

  • A social worker will make the decision to reach for the home stay
  • The decision is based on compatibility and the well-being of the youth.

How to sign up as a host?

These are the steps to take if you would like to volunteer as a host.

  1. To sign-up, click Sign-Up to go to the sign-up page.
  2. You will be asked to login with either an email account or Google account.
  3. You may select either login option.
